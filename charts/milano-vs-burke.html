Tarana Burke, founder of the Me Too movement, has had less global media attention than Alyssa Milano
Although interest in Burke has grown, coverage of Milano's viral #MeToo tweet makes her more popular overall. Burke has been covered about 80% as often as Milano.
Source: Media Cloud data as of Jan. 16, using the "ABYZ Global English Sources" collection, which contains around 1,500 sources publications listed in the ABYZ directory.
